August 2 (Day 1): Dehradun to Rishikesh

Activity: Travel to Rishikesh.

Duration: ~1 hr 21 mins (45.9 km) by road.

Overnight Stay: Rishikesh

Check: Finalize preparations, relax.

August 3 (Day 2): Rishikesh to Govindghat

Activity: Long drive to Govindghat.

Duration: 4-5 hours (250 km) by road.

Overnight Stay: Govindghat

Check: Pre-book taxi for comfort; be aware of limited mobile connectivity beyond Joshimath.

View Route on Map

August 4 (Day 3): Govindghat to Ghangaria

Activity: Trek to Ghangaria.

Duration: 5-6 hours (10 km trek after 4km motorable road).

Overnight Stay: Ghangaria

Check: Trek slowly for acclimatization; stay hydrated.

View Route on Map

August 5 (Day 4): Valley of Flowers (VoF)

Activity: Explore Valley of Flowers.

Duration: 5-6 hours (8 km round trip trek).

Overnight Stay: Ghangaria

Check: Start early (before 8 AM) for better views; obtain mandatory permit.

View Location on Map

August 6 (Day 5): Hemkund Sahib

Activity: Trek to Hemkund Sahib.

Duration: 6-9 hours (12 km round trip trek).

Overnight Stay: Ghangaria

Check: Very steep and challenging; consider pony for uphill; hydrate, slow pace, watch for AMS symptoms.

View Location on Map

August 7 (Day 6): Ghangaria to Badrinath

Activity: Trek to Govindghat, then drive to Badrinath.

Duration: Trek: 5-6 hours (10-11 km); Drive: ~33 mins (25.2 km).

Overnight Stay: Badrinath

Check: Proceed directly to Badrinath to optimize itinerary.

View Route on Map

August 8 (Day 7): Badrinath Exploration

Activity: Full day for Badrinath Temple darshan and local sightseeing.

Duration: Full Day.

Overnight Stay: Badrinath

Check: Enjoy the spiritual and scenic sites.

August 9 (Day 8): Badrinath to Rishikesh

Activity: Long drive back to Rishikesh.

Duration: ~5 hours (200+ km) by road.

Overnight Stay: Rishikesh

Check: Be prepared for potential monsoon-related delays.
